Number 1. Pasta with White Garlic Sauce

What do you need?

  • Pasta
  • Greek yogurt
  • Garlic
  • *Optional Rucola or mint leaves
  • Salt
  • Pot
  • Something to mix it with
  • Bowl
  • Grater (Very fine part)

How to make it?

  1. Boil some water and salt the water
  2. Chuck in any pasta of your choice (and wait however long the packaging says, mix it thoroughly, and keep it on low heat?

    3. Meanwhile lets make the sauce

  3. 3 tablespoons of Greek yogurt every 100g of Dry pasta/spaghetti
  4. For every 3 tablespoons of Yogurt, add 2 cloves of gralic minced finely
  5. Add a pinch of salt (20 grains)
  6. *Optional Add Some Rukola or Mint Leaves
  7. Mix it up!
  8. Drain your pasta water

    9. PSA: Recommended to not add oil, since the sauce will have a hard time sticking to the pasta

  9. Add the Sauce and Mix!

Number 2. Quick and easy Super fluffy Scrambled Eggs

What do you need?

  • Egg(s)
  • Salt
  • Butter
  • Bowl
  • Pan
  • Whisk/Fork
  • Silicon spatula whatever you'd like to use
  • A plate

How to make it?

  1. Get as many eggs as you would like and crack them in a bowl
  2. Mix them up with either a wisk or a fork (anything that works for you)
  3. On every 1 egg, add a tiny pich of salt (so for example 3 eggs, 3 tiny pinches of salt)
  4. Mix it up good!
  5. Now get a frying pan and heat it on high heat and put in butter (dont let it burn!)
  6. Get a Silicon spatula or anything that wont scracth the surface!
  7. Put in the eggs
  8. While cooking, Mix it
  9. The moment the eggs solidify, wait 3 seconds (And keep mixing)
  10. Put it on a plate and Done!
