History of the television

Originally created in the (late) 19-th century, it has become a staple peice of technology in our homes, modern TV's are used mainly for watching movies and playing games on your console. The first ever television was invented in 1884 by Paul Nipkow. Later on in the early 20-th century, CRT TV's became mainstream, CRT's existed for around 80 years, before Plasma LED/LCD TV's took over. LED/LCD TV's were the craze of the two-thousands, LED/LCD TV's replaced CRT's slowly because of its very high price, bt after a few years, prices lowered, and it became more mainstream. Now-a-Days we have pioneered TV tecgnology, Screens like: OLED, Mini-LED, QLED, QD-OLED and more...
